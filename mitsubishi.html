Mitsubishi

The Mitsubishi Group is a group of companies in Japan that have been known for their autonomous nature, diversity and technological innovation.

The Mitsubishi Group is made up of about 40 companies without a controlling parent company. Each of the Mitsubishi companies owns substantial shares in the others.

Lancer Evo X

The Lancer, one of the most iconic sports car series of all time, went out on a high note. The 10th-generation Lancer Evo was an AWD beast that made anywhere from 276hp to 440hp depending on the trim level.

3000GT VR-4

The 3000GT VR-4 was a relatively unknown car when it was released in 1990. Many people were shocked and delighted when they learned about Mitsubishi’s newest creation, which was at once a Grand Tourer and packed with features that were more commonly found in supercars of that time.
